Self-Directed Learning

Self-directed learning empowers individuals to take control of their own educational journey. It involves setting personal goals, identifying resources, and evaluating progress—placing the learner at the center of the experience.

This approach promotes critical thinking, motivation, and lifelong learning. Whether you're mastering a new skill or exploring a topic of passion, self-directed learning gives you the flexibility to learn at your own pace and on your own terms.

Embrace the freedom to learn what you want, when you want, and how you want—with tools and resources that support your individual learning style and goals.
