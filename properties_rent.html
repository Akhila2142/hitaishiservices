FAQs on Properties & Rent

Find answers to common questions from landlords and tenants about renting and leasing properties.

1. How is the monthly rent amount determined?

Rent is based on market trends, property features, amenities, and the landlord’s costs such as mortgage and upkeep.

2. What does a security deposit cover?

It covers unpaid rent, damage beyond normal wear, and any cleaning charges stated in the lease agreement.

3. Can the landlord increase rent during the lease term?

Not during the active lease term—rent changes usually apply only at renewal or with a new lease agreement.

4. Which utilities are typically included?

Water and garbage are often included. Electricity, internet, and gas are usually paid separately by tenants.

5. How do I request repairs?

Submit a written request to the landlord or manager. Urgent issues should be addressed within a reasonable timeframe.
